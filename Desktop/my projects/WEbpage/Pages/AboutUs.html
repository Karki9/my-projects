As a retail outlet and local authorized representatives for branded quality instruments, Premium Music Store focuses on, first: understanding and then, integrating musicians, event and production houses with their requirement.

Selling instruments and equipment may well be our most important function. But beyond the realm of undertaking a business, equally important to us is the notion of creating a positive environment for a local musical fraternity to develop. As more creative musical concepts and technical knowledge is exchanged, it allows us to correctly understand the requirement of both musicians and audience and develop on the future prospects of the market.

At Premium Music Store, we take the effort to attend various international music festivals to educate ourselves on the ever evolving scenario of music technology and to coordinate the process of developing the same concept in Nepal. We have different baranches all over the city. Visit us.
